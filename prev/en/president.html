白澤 賢斗

Kento Shirasawa

Career

Born on April 11, 2002, and lives in Ofunato City, Iwate Prefecture.
Hobbies are watching anime and movies, music, radio, games, making things, and learning languages.
Discovered the potential of technology and worked on regional promotion using IT in my teens.
Achieved the target amount by crowdfunding and participated in SXSW 2019.
Established Novalumo LLC in April 2020.

Greeting

Hi, I'm Shirasawa from Novalumo LLC. When I was an elementary school student, I started to get interested in various things since I came in touch with technology. I value a wide range of values, and I also try touching and checking what interests me. When I participated in SXSW when I was in my first year of high school, my life changed a lot. I love the locals, and I felt that “this place could be a wonderful place”, so I started various activities by taking advantage of what I got from my overseas experience. This was the first step in starting a business at the age of 18. I believe in all the possibilities of this world, including my own experience. As a "company that realizes its potential," we will take on various challenges regardless of the field, placing importance on relationships with people.
